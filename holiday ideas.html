Holiday Ideas

Explore India.

Hill
Stations

The hill stations of India welcome you with refreshing vacations for your friends and family. Dotted with wild flowers in full bloom, bright green beauty, apple orchards, waterfalls and unadulterated mountain air, all these hill stations present a pleasurable experience that you will remember for the rest of your life.

These hill stations in India are a must visit:

  • Darjeeling, West Bengal
  • Coorg, Karnataka
  • Manali, Himachal Pradesh
  • Munnar, Kerala
  • Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir

Heritage
Sites

India takes pride in flaunting its unsurpassed heritage; eras over eras have influenced, moulded and face lifted the rich heritage of which we all are part of. From the float of crocodiles at Sunderban National Park to the home of snow leopards at Nanda Devi Biosphere Reserve, from the majestic Manas Wildlife Sanctuary to the Kaziranga National Park, India humbly possess the most diverse heritage in the world.

A few heritage sites you can visit in India:

  • Kaziranga National Park, Assam
  • Humayun’s Tomb, Delhi
  • Hampi, Karnataka
  • Khajuraho, Madhya Pradesh
  • Ajanta and Ellora Caves, Maharashtra

Beaches



With over 7500 km stretch of coastline in its geographical treasure, India has always been an alluring destination when it comes to refreshing holidays away from the hustle and bustle of metropolitan life. Wind surfing, kayaking, scuba diving, snorkeling, water skiing, canoeing, angling and fishing are some of the adventure-filled activities that make beach tours in India more fruitful and mesmerizing.

Check out these breathtaking beaches from all across India:

  • Varkala Beach, Kerala
  • Goakarna Beach, Karnataka
  • Palolem Beach, Goa
  • Tarkarli Beach, Maharashtra
  • Radhanagar Beach, Havelock Island, Andaman Islands


Best places to visit during

Summer

Ladakh

The best way to reach Ladakh is by road. Either a drive from Manali or from Srinagar.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands

The islands are quite remote. Flight connections to Port Blair are available from selected airports, so are sea cruises.

Lakshadweep Islands

Kochi, Kerala is the gateway to the emerald islands of Lakshadweep.

Shillong, Meghalaya

Shillong Airport in Umroi is the closest airport. Bus services are also provided.

Gangtok, Sikkim

The closest airport is in Bagdogra and the nearest railway station in Jalpaiguri, West Bengal.

Best places to visit during

Winter

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

The Himalayan Toy Train is one of the best ways to arrive in the hill town. The nearest airport is in Chandigarh.

Pondicherry

Chennai International Airport, the nearest airport, is located 135 km away. Villupuram, the nearest railway station, is 35 km away.

Gulmarg, Kashmir

The nearest airport to Gulmarg is the Srinagar Airport.

Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh

The nearest airport to reach Tawang is Salonibari Airport in Tezpur, Assam.

Almora, Uttarakhand

Almora does not have its own airport but it is well connected with cities like Delhi and Dehradun by road. You can reach Almora from the Pantnagar airport.
